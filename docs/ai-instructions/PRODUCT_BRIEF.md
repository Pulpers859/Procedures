# Product Brief

## Concept

ProcedureSTAT is a rapid emergency medicine and ICU procedure-review app built in SwiftUI.

It helps clinicians refresh setup, anatomy, steps, troubleshooting, complications, and documentation before performing procedures.

## Primary users

- Emergency medicine residents
- Emergency medicine attendings
- Critical care fellows
- ICU clinicians

## Goals

- Useful review in 60–120 seconds
- Deeper review when time allows
- Standardized procedure format
- Fewer missed setup/equipment errors
- Clear complication and failure-plan review
- Resident education support

## Non-goals

The app is not:

- A credentialing product
- A substitute for formal procedural training
- A hospital policy replacement
- A billing/coding tool
- A real-time autonomous decision-maker
